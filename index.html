Minecrat

Minecraft is a popular sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios. Released in 2011, it allows players to explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world where they can mine resources, craft tools, build structures, and fight off enemies. The game has different modes, including Survival (where players must manage health and hunger) and Creative (which offers unlimited resources for freeform building).

Mario

Mario is one of the most iconic video game characters, created by Nintendo. First appearing in 1981 in Donkey Kong, Mario became the face of the Super Mario series, which launched in 1985 with Super Mario Bros. In these games, Mario, an Italian plumber, embarks on adventures to rescue Princess Peach from the villain Bowser.

Subway serf

Subway Surfers is a popular endless runner mobile game developed by Kiloo and SYBO Games. Released in 2012, players control a character who is caught spray-painting graffiti in a subway and must run through the train tracks to escape a pursuing inspector and his dog. Along the way, players collect coins, power-ups, and dodge oncoming trains and obstacles.
